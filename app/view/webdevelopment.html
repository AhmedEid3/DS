Website development is a form of art and you get a quality website, when there is a proper understanding of the medium. At VIBE Marketing we aptly harness the power of inventive creations and that of fast evolving internet technologies to deliver your project with highest quality standards. To understand web design, understanding internet is necessary.

Understanding the target audience is essential for us to be able to incorporate the design elements that will deliver a successful website. For example – A site that targets accountants is going to have a completely different feel from one targeting Window companies .

The basic web design plan includes:

  • Detailing information architecture
  • The planned site structure
  • A site map of the now to be designed and built pages
  • Various technical details. Such as use of scripts or Ajax, a server-side language like PHP or something else, need of a shopping cart and so on.
The designing process now begins where we determine:
  • Design Wire frames and the layout
  • Colour scheme and a harmonious combination
  • Planning a theme
  • Inclusion of decorative images and icons.
Content gathering is the next step because the visitors click on the site to look for certain content. The content can include text, images, and multimedia
  • Text – articles, lists, blog posts, reviews, etc.
  • Graphics –find images for web pages, photos, etc.
  • Multimedia – The use of multimedia should be done wisely.
